Who we are

A studio built on light, warmth, and considered detail.

At Ignite Interior Decors, we believe a well-designed room does more than look good — it changes how a space feels the moment you walk in.

Our designers blend natural materials, bold-yet-calming palettes, and smart spatial planning to create interiors that are equal parts liveable and unforgettable. From a single room refresh to a full home or commercial fit-out, we guide every project with clarity and care.

Good to know

Frequently asked.

Can't find your answer? Get in touch — we're happy to help.

How much does a project cost?

Every space is different, so we scope each project individually. After a free consultation we'll share a clear, itemised proposal — no hidden fees.

How long does a typical design take?

A single room refresh can take 4–6 weeks; a full home or commercial fit-out usually runs 3–6 months, depending on scope and construction.

Do you only take on large projects?

Not at all. From styling a single living room to a whole-building fit-out, we tailor our involvement to what you need.

Can we use our own furniture?

Absolutely. We love blending cherished pieces with new finds to create interiors that feel authentically yours.
